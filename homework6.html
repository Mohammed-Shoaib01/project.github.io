French Toast

French Toast is an amazing yet easy breakfast snack that can be made with simple ingredients.

It can be made as a breakfast dish for your morning yummy meals!!

How to make French Toast

You'll need the following ingredients:

  • Fresh Bread
  • Eggs
  • Sugar

Make French Toast now using the recipe below!! :

  1. Wisk the eggs. 🥚
  2. Using a bread knife slice the bread into medium size slices. 🔪🍞
  3. Use sugar as needed and mix it well with bread slices. 😋🤤
  4. Now dip the bread into egg and cover the slice properly. 😍
  5. Fry both sides with minimum required oil/butter and enjoy!🍴😊
